COOKIES POLICY

  1. 1. What are cookies and why do we use them on the website?

    A cookie is a file that is downloaded in the user’s device to access a certain website for storing and recovering information about the browsing taking place in the computer.

    Cookies enable this website, among other things, to store and recover information about the user’s options and habits. The cookies used on this website are necessary in order for it to function.

    It is important to remember that the use of cookies does not imply that the user’s personal data are provided.

  2. 2. What kinds of cookies exist?

    Cookies can be divided into session or persistent cookies, depending on the time they remain activated:

    • Session cookies: The data included and stored by these cookies are collected while the user browses the website and are deleted when the browser is closed. They are normally used to store information that should be kept to render the service requested by the user on a single occasion.

    • Persistent cookies: By means of these cookies, the data continue being stored in the terminal for a certain period of time; hence they allow the chosen preferences to be controlled without needing to repeat certain parameters every time the website is visited.

    In addition, cookies can be classified as proprietary or third-party, depending on the company that manages them:

    • First-party cookies: These cookies are sent to the user’s terminal equipment from equipment or a domain managed by the editor and from where the service requested by the user is rendered.

    • Third-party cookies: These cookies are sent to the user’s terminal equipment from equipment or a domain not managed by the editor but by another entity that processes the data obtained from the cookies.

    Regarding the purpose, the cookies can be:

    • Technical cookies: These cookies enable the data traffic and communication to be controlled.

    • Customization cookies: These cookies allow users access depending on some of their characteristics that are collected (browser, language, etc.).

    • Analytical cookies: These cookies enable the person responsible for them to monitor and analyze the behavior of website users and allow a user profile to be created.

    • Advertising Cookies: These cookies collect data about the management of advertising spaces.
