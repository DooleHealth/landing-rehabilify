what is the problem?

The REHABILIFY solution offers a dual-system-based approach. On one side, a patient management center to all the involved professionals, following an integrated-care approach and incorporating a system to support decision-making. On the other hand, a patient with rehabilitation needs will benefit from an application that promotes self-management, behavioral change, and better follow-up.

REHABILIFY provides patients with intelligent support through suitable recommender systems focused on the main pillars of health adapted to each use-case and a managed digital community to provide peer support and motivation. To provide collaboration, cooperation, and continuous patients’ monitoring and follow-up, professionals have access to a management system that includes decision support systems for risk stratification and care plan definition. The REHABILIFY solution will be ready for integration with third-party solutions and devices thanks to an open portal that enables and supports a unique access point for prescription of remote rehabilitation solutions.

what is REHABILIFY

Rehabilitation is a complex and vital process as part of the continuum of care for those seeking to regain or maintain their quality of life and improve function following significant injury or illness. Rehabilitation can reduce disability, improve function and quality of life, and provide cost-effective benefits in the short and long term. However, rehabilitation needs professional long-term follow-up1, and often daily intervention. This is usually done at specialised/central/referral hospitals, which are often far from the patients’ rural or isolated homes. Failing to provide some form of rehabilitative service will increase morbidity and mortality; in areas where rehabilitative services are not available there is a bigger and increasing demand of acute care and long-term care services. Demand for rehabilitation services is growing and it is expected to continue to be in high demand due to the ageing population, improvements in healthcare (survival rates from catastrophic injuries is higher than ever), and new technologies and treatments.

  • TPS is registered in a single Rehabilitify instance, completely separate and independent from the telemedicine platform, which is shared across the ecosystem.
  • TPS reaches an agreement with each individual procurer.
  • TPS is recommended or prescribed by professionals based on the patient's pathology and patient's profile.
  • The Rehabilitify app offers functionality from mixed multiple sources:

    -Adherence: gamification, games.

    -TPS Source: add information via soft / Deep integration.

  • Deep Integration offers to rehabilify app to show forms from different origins at the same screen.
  • From TPS is possible to send to Rehabilify:

    • -push notifications.

    -metrics.

    -mail.

INTEGRATION
  • Necessary Steps to Register:

-App is released to procurers instance.

-TPS registers as developer.

-TPS uploads app.

-Rehabilify approves app.

-TPS reaches agreement with Procurer.

-Procurer authorizes publication.

  • TPS can register in the Developers Rehabilify, using the following link:

https://salud.rehabilify.com/rehabilify/register

  • From the Developers Portal, Rehabilify Dev > App Store, it's possible for the TPS to:

-Register and maintain apps.

-View usage metrics.

-Manage their users.

-Find support information.

-View legal agreements.

  • When a professional prescribes the solution, the TPS is called via API to enroll it.
  • To integrate TPS into APP, it's possible to choose between 2 types of integration:

-Soft integration: TPS appears on the patient's home page. When patient clicks, it’s possible to choose between 2 options:

-iFrame: the app opens within Rehabilitify.

-Native app: the app is opened on the user's device.

-Deep integration: TPS publishes information directly in Rehabilitify.

Architecture & Security

All technological components developed in the REHABILIFY platform are developed using Open-Source Software and Services. This feature is already supported by the Doole Platform and EURECAT’s XCARE Data Platform.

REHABILIFY platform provides:

  • A tier for presentation / interfacing, a tier for business logic and a tier for data are features supported by the Rehabilify Platform.
  • Automated deployment mechanisms to grant fast installations and setup procedures.
  • Audit mechanisms to log user interaction with the platform, including access attempts and transactions or specific operations on data registries.

REHABILIFY enables:

  • Software services logs for audit and forensic purposes.
  • HPKP and two-way authentication for security enhancement.
  • Process tracking auditing.

REHABILIFY verifies a user’s session upon logout via access token management.
